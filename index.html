Our Story

About Our Church

City Revival Center Church – River of Life is a vibrant, Spirit-filled congregation devoted to advancing the Kingdom of God. We are a people who believe in signs, wonders, and miracles — and we have seen the hand of God move in extraordinary ways.

Our mission is simple: to reach the lost, disciple the saved, and send the equipped. We believe every person was created with a God-given purpose, and it is our joy to help you discover and walk in that calling.

We are passionate about prayer, bold in evangelism, and committed to genuine community. As the River of Life flows, we carry that current of God's love into every neighborhood, home, and heart we touch.
